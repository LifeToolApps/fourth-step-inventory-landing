Privacy Policy

Fourth Step Inventory
Effective date: January 9, 2026

Summary

Fourth Step Inventory is a private journaling tool. Your inventory entries are stored locally on your device. The app does not require an account and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect data.

  • No analytics
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No tracking
  • No account or sign-in

Data Storage

Any text you enter (e.g., resentments, affected areas, character defects) is stored locally on your device. If you choose to export your inventory (JSON/CSV), the exported file is created on your device and is under your control.

Network Access

The app does not upload your inventory to any server. If you open external links (for example, this website), those requests are handled by your browser and device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Your Control

You can delete your inventory by deleting the app from your device. Exported files can be deleted like any other file.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: 4thstepapp@gmail.com
