Fourth Step Inventory

Fourth Step Inventory is a private journaling app designed to help users examine resentments, patterns, and character defects using a structured Fourth Step–style inventory.

All entries are stored locally on your device. The app does not transmit, upload, or share any personal data.

This app is inspired by traditional 12-step inventory practices but is not affiliated with Alcoholics Anonymous or any other fellowship.

Privacy Policy

Fourth Step Inventory does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data outside of your device.

All information you enter into the app is stored locally on your device using standard system storage. The app does not use analytics, tracking, advertising identifiers, or third-party services.

The app does not require account creation, sign-in, or network access to function.

Because data is stored locally, privacy depends on the security of your device. If others have physical or digital access to your device, they may be able to access app data.

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer at the email address listed above.
